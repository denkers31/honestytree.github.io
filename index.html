Honesty Tree

Grow through honesty.

No Cloud. Ever.

Honesty Tree is a private, daily moral inventory designed to help you reflect honestly and grow steadily—one day at a time. Inspired by the principles of Step Ten, it provides a simple, structured way to review your day without judgment.

What Honesty Tree Offers

  • Daily guided moral inventory
  • Judgment-free reflection
  • Pattern awareness and accountability
  • Offline-first, private design
  • Optional sharing initiated only by you

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Honesty Tree is built with privacy as a core principle.

Data Collection

Honesty Tree does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data. All entries are created and remain locally on your device.

Data Storage

Your data is stored only on your device. Honesty Tree does not use cloud services, external servers, analytics, or tracking of any kind.

Third Parties

Honesty Tree does not integrate with third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics providers.

Sharing

Any sharing of content is explicitly initiated by you using your device’s built-in sharing features. Honesty Tree does not automatically send data anywhere.

Data Deletion

You may delete your data at any time by removing entries within the app or by uninstalling the app.

Children’s Privacy

Honesty Tree does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be made available on this page.
